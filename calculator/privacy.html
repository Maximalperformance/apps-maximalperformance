Support Privacy Policy

Overview

The Maximal Performance Bulk / Cut Calculator ("the app") is operated by Maximal Performance ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying information.

Any data you enter into the app (bodyweight, height, age, activity level, goal) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS local storage. This data remains on your device and is removed if you delete the app. We have no access to this data at any time.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any equivalent service within the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is intended for users aged 13 and over.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@maximalperformance.nz.
